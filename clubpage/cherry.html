Cherry Networks

At Cherry+ Network, they aspire to be the leading community that empowers students to forge professional connections and hone their skills to become proficient in their respective domains. They envision a future where every student has the opportunity to expand their professional horizons through creative networking events, hands-on workshops, and insightful discussions, all within a supportive and thriving community.

Cherry+ Networks comprises of 2 bodies:
1. ENDEAVOUR

Endeavour, the heart of Cherry+ Network, is the stage to break your limits, explore the infinite possibilities provided by our elite domains and embark on the journey of skill development to be the difference between a cherry in the bush and a cherry at the top.

Team Domains are:-

  • Events
  • Desgin
  • Corporate

2. TECHVERSE

Ignite your technical prowess! Showcase skills, explore tech fields, and join a community of techies. Embark on a tech journey. Showcase skills, explore, innovate, and connect with fellow tech enthusiasts. Unleash your tech potential. Build, innovate, and learn alongside like-minded tech enthusiasts.

Team Domains are:-

  • Web Development
  • App Development
  • UI/UX
  • Innovation
