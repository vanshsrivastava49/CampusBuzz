Movies and Dramatics Club

The club collaborates with young entertainers to celebrate various aspects of the performing arts. It welcomes students on board who are engrossed to explore more about the thespian world. It aims at finding out the innate artistic talents among students, nurturing them to their fullest, and providing a platform for students who have the talent, confidence and passion to do something unique. Drama Club promotes communication skills, teamwork, dialogue, socialisation, stages terminology, and working on acting skills.
