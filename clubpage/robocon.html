SRM Team Robocon

Team Robocon is the oldest robotics team of SRM University, also heralded as one of the fastest growing teams in the country. At the onset of 98th National Science Congress, group of students who won the Best Innovation title by DRDO formed SRM Team Robocon. We currently work with and under a broad umbrella of ideas and concepts, primarily related to robotics and are involved in developing mobile robotic systems.
