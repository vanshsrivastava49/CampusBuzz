SRM WOMEN EMPOWERMENT CLUB

Women Empowerment Club at SRM University has emerged as a beacon of unwavering commitment to fostering awareness and catalyzing change. With a meticulous approach, the club has strategically orchestrated a series of events and impactful campaigns, creating a ripple effect that extends far beyond the confines of the campus.Through its dynamic initiatives, the club has consistently been at the forefront of promoting women's empowerment, weaving a tapestry of inclusivity and strength within the campus community. The carefully planned events serve as powerful platforms, sparking crucial conversations and encouraging a collective shift in mindset.
