Data Science Community

Data Science Community is a student-led, technology-driven Community where we create practical projects, carry out research, and plan meet ups and events. As a team, DSC SRM has a lot of accomplishments to be proud of, including placing in the top four in the Smart India Hackathon, winning VIT Hack 2020, receiving offers for summer internships from Walmart, Google, and Mitacs, taking home the gold medal at the SRM Research Day in 2021, winning Hack Yuva, placing fourth in Major League Hacking, and many more.
